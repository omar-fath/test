Academic Difference

  • Receive Dual Spanish Master of Business Management (MBM) Diplomas upon graduation. One from an accredited business school, another from a leading University.

    • International Business School in Barcelona (ESEI), its MBM program is ranked among the top MBM programs in Spain.

    • Catholic University of Murcia (UCAM), is ranked 10th in Europe for teaching quality according to Times Higher Education (THE) Ranking.

  • Certificate of completion for every course completed from Cairo Graduate College (CGC). CGC is a member of ACQUIN e.v., a distinguished German accrediting institution.

  • Complete your MBM in 1 academic year - 9 months (October-June).

  • Case method, experiential learning, student centered learning and Project based learning allow our students to build deep general management and leadership skills.

  • 50% of the courses are taught online by prominent international faculty who are renowned academic scholars and leading industry experts.

  • 50% of the courses are taught offline by exceptional national faculty who are renowned academic scholars and leading industry experts.

  • CGC-ESEI Dual MBM students have access to an online library that has a wide selection of more than 810,000 eBooks and volumes, and thousands of peer-reviewed journals and academic research papers.

  • CGC Dual MBM students will have access to yearly professional and educational study tours in Spain.

Career Path Development

  • CGC-ESEI Dual MBM helps you with your career progression in post COVID-19 world by offering:

    • Yearly employment fair with tens of our recruitment partners in Egypt and Spain

    • Help with the interview and job placement processes

    • Equipping you with the much-needed business, management and leadership skills that fit your goals.

Admissions

  • Limited number of selected students will be admitted per cohort
  • CGC-ESEI Dual MBM program’s acceptance rate is less than 10%
  • 2 intakes per academic year: Fall (October) and spring (February) cohorts.
  • Online classes are offered after working hours.
  • Offline classes are offered on Saturdays.

AlumniCommunity

  • Join an expanding and diverse international and national network of thousands of alumni. ESEI and CGC alumni are leading industries in more than 30 countries.

Entrepreneurship at CGC

  • CGC Dual MBM students and alumni are eligible to apply for the CGC incubator
  • CGC incubator offers its startups seed funding, workspace, networking, mentorship and trainings to launch and grow their businesses. CGC incubator offers funding of up to EGP 100,000 in exchange for a minor equity stake of up to 15% to promising selected startups upon graduation.

Women at CGC Dual MBM

  • We prepare women for leadership roles by equipping them with the needed tools to accelerate careers and achieve goals.
  • Female applicants are eligible to apply for the merit-based women leadership scholarship
